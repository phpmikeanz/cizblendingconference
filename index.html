About the Conference

Conference and Blending for all churches in Southern Philippines

Dear Brothers and Sisters in the Lord

The Church in Zamboanga City will be holding a conference and blending this coming December 13-14, 2025, which will be held in KCC CONVENTION CENTER. This event is open to all churches. However, due to limited space, registration is on a first-come first-served basis. Therefore, early registration is appreciably encouraged. While we love your children, we are sorry that we are unable to accommodate them in this occasion.

Conference Highlights

Ministry of the Word

Enjoy rich sharing from the ministry of Watchman Nee and Witness Lee, focusing on God's eternal purpose, the experience of Christ as life, and the practical building up of the Body of Christ.

Church Life & Blending

Participate in blending among the churches according to the practice of the New Testament, for the building up of the Body in oneness and reality.

Prophesying & Sharing

All saints are encouraged to speak, prophesy, and share Christ for mutual encouragement and edification‚Äîaccording to 1 Corinthians 14.
